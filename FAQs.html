1. What is a Solar Rooftop System ?
keyboard_arrow_down
In a solar rooftop system , the solar panels are installed in the roof of any residential, commercial, institutional and industrial buildings.

This can be of two types
(i) Solar Rooftop System with storage facility using battery.
(ii) Grid Connected Solar Rooftop System.

3. How much roof area is required to set up rooftop Solar System ?
keyboard_arrow_down
About 10 sq.m or 120 sq.ft. area is required to set up 1 kWp grid connected rooftop solar system.
4. What is Net-Metering ?
keyboard_arrow_down
The grid connected rooftop system can work on net metering basis wherein the beneficiary pays to the utility on net meter reading basis only.
5. How to apply for solar rooftop subsidy ?
keyboard_arrow_down
When an authorized vendor like SolarQube installs your solar panels, its the vendor that applies for the subsidy on your behalf.
You have to pay for the system's price minus the subsidy amount.
